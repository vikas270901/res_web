  • 1) Fill in your details. Fields marked with (*) are mandatory.

    2) After filling the form, “sign up”.

    3) A conformation email would be sent to the email id provided by you in the registration form.

    4) Click on the link provided in the email and you will receive your “login id” and “password”, through which you may login subsequently.

    5) You can view your profile from “My Profile” option.

    • ALUMNI MEET
    • JIIT Alumni Meet 14th Feb 2015 JIIT held its maiden Alumni Meet (for JIIT & JBS both) on 14th Feb 2015. The event in itself was an impressive one. It was indeed great to have more than 160 Alumni participants from all pass out batches. The Event started at 05:30PM with a warm welcome to all our Alumni by Hon’ble Vice Chancellor, Prof. S.C Saxena through his enlightening words followed by spectacular cultural performances (Dance, singing, instrumental and mimicry etc) given by our present students of JIIT. There was a brief tea-break in – between and the Alumni enjoyed wonderful interaction with VC , HOD’s & Professors and re-lived the nostalgic moments of their JIIT days. Soon after the tea break, event again resumed with some of the Alumni sharing stage to present their feelings on being back to Alma-Mater and their achievements in professional front. A sharp Quiz round added more fun to the event. Priyam Kotwal (Biotechnology, 2014 batch Pass out) gave a melodious singing performance on guitar. With these memorable moments, the event concluded with a vote of thanks by JIIT Team to the Alumni followed by sumptuous Dinner and take away goodies.
